# Privacy Policy

**SpaceMint**
Last updated: April 2026

---

## Who We Are

SpaceMint is a personal storage management app built for Android. 
It was designed with one goal — to help people clean their phones 
slowly and mindfully, without stress or risk.

This app was built independently. There is no company behind it. 
Just a developer who wanted to solve a real problem.

---

## The Short Version

Your files stay on your phone.
Your data stays on your phone.
Nothing leaves your device. Ever.

---

## What SpaceMint Can See

When you grant permission, SpaceMint can read the list of photos, 
videos and files stored on your device. This is necessary to show 
you files for review during each session.

SpaceMint reads file names, sizes and dates to display them to you. 
It does not copy, upload, analyse or transmit any of this information 
anywhere.

---

## What SpaceMint Cannot See

- Your photos are never uploaded to any server
- Your videos are never processed outside your device
- Your personal information is never collected
- Your usage patterns are never tracked or sold
- SpaceMint has no internet permission in its core function

---

## Permissions We Use

**Read Media Images and Read Media Video**
Used only to display your photos and videos inside the app 
so you can decide whether to keep or delete them. These files 
never leave your device.

**Post Notifications**
Used to send you two daily reminders — a morning one and an 
evening one. You can turn these off anytime in your phone settings.

**Schedule Exact Alarm**
Used to ensure your reminders arrive at the time you set, 
not whenever the system feels like it.

**Request Ignore Battery Optimizations**
Used to keep reminders working reliably on devices that 
aggressively manage background processes.

---

## Data We Store Locally

SpaceMint saves a small amount of data on your device only:

- Your notification time preferences
- Your daily clean target setting
- Your session history — how many files reviewed and deleted
- Your day streak count
- A list of file IDs already shown to you, so files do not repeat

This data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences system. 
It lives only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

---

## File Deletion

When you choose to delete a file through SpaceMint, it is removed 
from your device using Android's official MediaStore system. 
SpaceMint does not keep any copy of deleted files. 
Deletion requires your explicit confirmation every time.

---

## Third Party Services

SpaceMint does not use:

- Google Analytics
- Firebase
- Facebook SDK
- Any advertising network
- Any crash reporting service
- Any data collection tool of any kind

## Children

SpaceMint does not knowingly collect information from anyone, 
including children. There is nothing to collect.

preferences are removed automatically.
